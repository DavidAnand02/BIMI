Meet BIMI: The Fact-Checking Chatbot You Can Trust

In a world overwhelmed by misinformation, BIMI is here to set the record straight. Powered by the trusted Google Fact Check Explorer, BIMI taps into a global network of IFCN-accredited fact-checkers and other reputable organizations to ensure you receive the most reliable, accurate information available.

About BIMI

At BIMI, we’re committed to empowering you with the truth. Our fact-checking chatbot is designed to provide fast, unbiased, and reliable information verified by trusted sources like the International Fact-Checking Network (IFCN).

Why BIMI?

  • Fast & Reliable: Get accurate information in real-time from verified fact-checking databases.
  • IFCN-Backed: BIMI consults trusted sources accredited by the International Fact-Checking Network (IFCN), alongside other respected fact-checking organizations.
  • Unbiased Sources: BIMI works exclusively with credible, independent fact-checkers committed to transparency and factual reporting.
  • Effortless Verification: No more endless Googling—BIMI simplifies fact-checking to a single conversation.

How It Works

  • Ask BIMI: Provide the claim you’d like to verify.
  • Fact-Check in Action: BIMI consults the Google Fact Check Explorer, pulling data from reputable, IFCN-accredited sources.
  • Truth Delivered: Receive a clear and concise response with links to verified sources.

Your Ally Against Misinformation

With BIMI, you don’t have to take things at face value. Equip yourself with the facts, and make informed decisions confidently. You can press the blue icon in the bottom right corner to activate BIMI or either of the "Start Fact-Checking Now" buttons.
